I’m a passionate singer and songwriter whose resilience was built through faith, family, and self-discipline. My strength comes from my mother’s example, growing up singing in church, and pushing myself to turn hard work into habit. I’ve learned to turn past insecurities into generosity and motivation, using music as my outlet and my purpose. My growth comes from self-critique, support from loved ones, and stepping out of my comfort zone to explore new sounds and skills. I believe in taking risks, staying consistent, and always striving to be the best version of myself. My journey is fueled by passion, faith, and the belief that every step forward counts.
